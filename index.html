✨🍫 Chocolate Cake 🍰✨

⏱ Prep Time: 45 mins

🍽 Serves: 6 cuties

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes. Enjoy!
